A Species Taken From Their Home

One day a robotic fleet appears above your planet. In mere seconds they descend, capturing every Starfish on the surface.

Locked in a cage, another Starfish manages to escape and frees you. The attempt and escape, but the defense system of the ship notices and takes aim. Your savior sees the danger and, in an act of self-sacrifice, pushes you out of the way just in time.

As they perish, they leave you their remaining power. The influx of energy changes your very DNA, turning you into a Squid-like creature: the first Celestialpod.

With their sacrifice fresh in your mind, you charge towards the fleet to save the rest of the Starfish! Defeat robotic enemies reminiscent of forgotten species, and discover the chilling reality of what could befall your friends!
